## 🎯 Decision Framework: Which Strategy?

### Choose Sequential If:
- ✅ Small development team (< 5 developers available)
- ✅ Very risk-averse culture
- ✅ Want to validate each CR before next
- ✅ Budget released yearly, not upfront

### Choose Parallel Pairs If: ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- ✅ Medium team (5-10 developers available)
- ✅ Want balance of speed and risk
- ✅ Can coordinate multiple teams
- ✅ Want CR synergies (feedback + baseline, resource + hierarchical)

### Choose Maximum Parallelization If:
- ✅ Large team (12-15 developers available)
- ✅ Urgent business need (competitive pressure)
- ✅ Experienced with multi-project execution
- ✅ Full budget available upfront

### Choose Phased Value If:
- ✅ Need to prove ROI before continued funding
- ✅ Budget approved phase-by-phase
- ✅ Want flexibility to pivot based on results
- ✅ Prioritize quick wins and validation
