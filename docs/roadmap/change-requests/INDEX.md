## рҹҺҜ Decision Framework: Which Strategy?

### Choose Sequential If:
- вң… Small development team (< 5 developers available)
- вң… Very risk-averse culture
- вң… Want to validate each CR before next
- вң… Budget released yearly, not upfront

### Choose Parallel Pairs If: вӯҗ RECOMMENDED
- вң… Medium team (5-10 developers available)
- вң… Want balance of speed and risk
- вң… Can coordinate multiple teams
- вң… Want CR synergies (feedback + baseline, resource + hierarchical)

### Choose Maximum Parallelization If:
- вң… Large team (12-15 developers available)
- вң… Urgent business need (competitive pressure)
- вң… Experienced with multi-project execution
- вң… Full budget available upfront

### Choose Phased Value If:
- вң… Need to prove ROI before continued funding
- вң… Budget approved phase-by-phase
- вң… Want flexibility to pivot based on results
- вң… Prioritize quick wins and validation
