# ADPA Marketing Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Your First Week Action Plan

### Day 1: LinkedIn Article
**Time:** 30 minutes

1. Copy `linkedin-article.md` content
2. Log into LinkedIn
3. Click "Write article" (not a post!)
4. Paste content
5. Add 3-5 relevant images:
   - Complexity Score screenshot
   - Before/after comparison
   - Process flow diagram
   - Team photo (optional)
   - Results dashboard
6. Add these hashtags at bottom:
   `#AI #ProjectManagement #BusinessAnalysis #EnterpriseAI #Innovation`
7. Publish!
8. Share article as a post with teaser:
   ```
   Just published: How we reduced document generation from 7 hours to 43 seconds.
   
   Real numbers. Real results. 600x faster, 99.998% cheaper.
   
   [Link to article]
   
   What documentation challenges are you facing? 👇
   ```

### Day 2: Medium Setup + Cross-Post
**Time:** 20 minutes

1. Create Medium account (or Substack)
2. Import LinkedIn article
3. Add "Originally published on LinkedIn" note
4. Add your email capture form
5. Publish
6. Share on LinkedIn: "Also available on Medium for those who prefer..."

### Day 3: LinkedIn Short Post #1
**Time:** 10 minutes

Use the short post from `linkedin-post.md`:
- Copy content
- Add Complexity Score image
- Post
- Engage with comments for 15 minutes after posting

### Day 4: Engagement Day
**Time:** 30 minutes

- Find 10 posts about project management/documentation challenges
- Leave thoughtful comments (3-5 sentences each)
- Connect with authors who respond
- Message: "Saw your post about [topic]. Really resonated. Would love to connect!"

### Day 5: LinkedIn Short Post #2
**Time:** 10 minutes

Create a simple post:
```
Quick question for PMs and BAs:

What % of your time is spent on documentation vs actual analysis/strategy?

Curious to hear real numbers. Comment below 👇

[Poll: <10% | 10-25% | 25-40% | >40%]
```

### Day 6-7: Community Engagement
**Time:** 20 minutes each day

- Join 3-5 LinkedIn groups (Project Management, Business Analysis)
- Answer 5 questions in groups
- Share your article in relevant groups (if allowed)
- Connect with 10 new people in your target audience

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Week 1 Pitfalls:

**Mistake #1: Post and Ghost**
- ❌ Publish article, never engage with comments
- ✅ Spend 2x as much time engaging as posting

**Mistake #2: Selling Too Hard**
- ❌ "ADPA is amazing! Sign up now!"
- ✅ "Here's what we learned building ADPA. Thoughts?"

**Mistake #3: Inconsistent Posting**
- ❌ 5 posts Monday, nothing for 2 weeks
- ✅ 2-3 posts per week, every week

**Mistake #4: Ignoring DMs**
- ❌ Wait 24-48 hours to respond
- ✅ Respond within 2 hours (shows you care)

**Mistake #5: Talking Features, Not Benefits**
- ❌ "ADPA has multi-provider AI with caching"
- ✅ "Save 7.2 hours per document, free up 3 FTEs"

## 📈 Scaling From Week 5+

Once you've validated the content works (Week 1-4), scale by:

### 1. Increase Frequency
- Week 1-4: 2-3 posts/week
- Week 5-8: 3-5 posts/week
- Week 9+: 5-7 posts/week (daily)

### 2. Add Video
- Record 5-7 minute demo
- Create 60-second reels
- Host LinkedIn Live Q&A
- Start YouTube channel

### 3. Build Email List
- Create lead magnet: "ROI Calculator" or "Documentation Audit Checklist"
- Collect emails through LinkedIn articles
- Weekly newsletter with insights

### 4. Partner Content
- Guest post on other blogs
- Podcast appearances
- Co-webinars with complementary tools
- PMI/IIBA chapter presentations

### 5. Consider Paid
- Only after organic is working
- Start with $1,000/month test budget
- LinkedIn Sponsored Content
- Retarget article readers
