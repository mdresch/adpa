# **1. Executive Summary** *(300 words)*

**EcoTrack** is a **next-generation Smart Sustainability Management Platform** designed to empower **EU-based manufacturing enterprises (500–5,000 employees)** to achieve **regulatory compliance, operational efficiency, and measurable sustainability impact**. The platform integrates **real-time IoT sensor data, AI-driven analytics, and automated reporting** to streamline **carbon tracking, energy optimization, waste reduction, and ESG compliance** under **EU Taxonomy, CSRD, GHG Protocol, and ISO 14001** standards.

### **Purpose of the Communications Management Plan**

This **Communications Management Plan (CMP)** defines the **who, what, when, why, and how** of all project communications for **EcoTrack**, ensuring:

- **Stakeholder alignment** with project objectives and regulatory requirements.

- **Timely, accurate, and transparent** information flow across all levels (executive, technical, operational).

- **Compliance** with EU reporting mandates (e.g., CSRD deadlines, ESG disclosures).

- **Risk mitigation** through structured escalation paths and issue resolution protocols.

- **Knowledge retention** via documented decisions, lessons learned, and change logs.

### **Key Objectives**

| **Objective**                          | **Success Metric**                                                                 | **Target Date**       |
|----------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Ensure 100% compliance with CSRD reporting | All mandatory disclosures submitted on time (Q1 2026)                            | 2026-03-31            |
| Achieve 95% stakeholder satisfaction    | Quarterly survey score ≥4.5/5 (Executives, Pilot Partners, End Users)              | Ongoing               |
| Maintain <5% project documentation errors | Audit of 100% of formal records (e.g., change requests, meeting minutes)          | 2025-12-31            |
| Reduce communication-related delays by 30% | Track delays in decision-making (baseline: 15% of issues in Q1 2025 → target: 10%) | 2026-06-30            |

### **Scope**

This plan covers **all project phases** (Initiation → Closure) and **all stakeholder groups**, including:

- **Executive Leadership** (C-level, Sponsor, Investors)

- **Regulatory Bodies** (EU ESG Auditor, European Environment Agency)

- **Technical Teams** (Architects, Developers, IoT Engineers)

- **End Users** (Shop Floor Workers, Facility Managers)

- **External Partners** (IoT Vendors, ESG Software Providers, Pilot Manufacturers)

## **3.2 Critical Communication Paths**

*Key dependencies mapped to **PMBOK 7 Project Work Performance Domain**.*

1. **Regulatory Compliance Chain**:
   `Sustainability Lead → EU ESG Auditor → Legal Counsel → Sponsor`
   - **Trigger**: New EU Taxonomy guidance.
   - **Flow**: Draft interpretation → Legal review → Auditor validation → Sponsor approval.
   - **SLA**: 10 business days.

2. **Technical Escalation Path**:
   `Dev Team → Tech Lead → CISO → CCB`
   - **Trigger**: Security vulnerability (e.g., IoT sensor data breach).
   - **Flow**: Incident report → Risk assessment → Mitigation plan → CCB approval.
   - **SLA**: 48 hours for critical issues.

3. **Pilot Partner Feedback Loop**:
   `End Users → Business Analyst → Product Manager → Tech Lead`
   - **Trigger**: UAT defect or usability issue.
   - **Flow**: Log in Jira → Triage → Prioritize → Sprint backlog.
   - **SLA**: 5 business days for high-priority items.

# **6. Risk and Issue Communication**

## **6.1 Escalation Protocol**

*Integrated with **Risk Register (v1.0, 2025-03-10)**.*

| **Risk Level** | **Trigger**                                  | **Initial Action**                          | **Escalation Path**                                      | **SLA**               |
|----------------|---------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|------------------------|
| **Critical**   | Compliance breach (e.g., CSRD non-compliance)| Immediate containment (Sustainability Lead) | CISO → Sponsor → CCB (within 4 hours)                   | 24 hours               |
| **High**       | Budget overrun >10%                         | Root-cause analysis (Project Manager)      | Finance Director → CCB (within 24 hours)                | 48 hours               |
| **Medium**     | Vendor delivery delay                       | Mitigation plan (Finance Director)          | Tech Lead → Project Manager (within 48 hours)           | 5 business days        |
| **Low**        | Minor UAT feedback                          | Log in Jira (Business Analyst)              | Sprint backlog review (next planning session)          | 10 business days       |

## **6.2 Issue Resolution Workflow**

1. **Log**: Issue captured in Jira (or MS Teams for urgent items).
2. **Triage**: Owner assigned based on **RACI matrix** (see Stakeholder Register).
3. **Investigate**: Root cause identified (e.g., technical debt, regulatory gap).
4. **Propose**: Solutions documented with cost/impact analysis.
5. **Approve**: CCB for high-impact; Tech Lead for medium; Owner for low.
6. **Implement**: Changes deployed in next sprint/release.
7. **Close**: Verification by requester; Update lessons learned.
