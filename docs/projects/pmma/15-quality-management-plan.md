## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This Quality Management Plan (QMP) defines how the team achieves quality objectives for the Project Management Mastery Accelerator (PMMA). Aligned with PMBOK 7 principles and the Quality Performance Domain, this plan ensures PMMA delivers its transformative vision of creating a dynamic, data-driven "flight simulator" for project management.

## 7. Continuous Improvement

### 7.1 Improvement Process

1. **Identify Opportunities**: Through QA/QC activities, feedback, metrics analysis
2. **Develop Plans**: Scope, objectives, and implementation approach
3. **Implement**: Execute improvement plans
4. **Monitor Results**: Validate effectiveness
5. **Integrate**: Incorporate successful improvements into processes
