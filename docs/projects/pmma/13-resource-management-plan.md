## 4. Resource Acquisition

### 4.1 Internal Staffing

1. Identify Needs: PM submits resource request form
2. Approval: Resource Manager and Functional Managers review
3. Onboarding: HR facilitates access provisioning and training

### 4.2 External Staffing

1. Vendor Selection: Procurement issues RFP and evaluates proposals
2. Contract Negotiation: Legal & Compliance reviews contracts
3. Onboarding: PM integrates external resources into team

### 4.3 Release Criteria

- Completion of deliverables
- Knowledge transfer to remaining team
- Formal acceptance by Project Manager

---

## 5. Team Development

### 5.1 Training Needs

- Semantic Analysis (ADPA and ECS modeling)
- Graph Databases (Neo4j certification)
- Agile Methodologies (Scrum and Kanban)
- Governance Frameworks (PMBOK 7, compliance standards)

### 5.2 Team Agreements

- **Communication**: Daily stand-ups, bi-weekly sprint reviews, monthly retrospectives
- **Conflict Resolution**: Escalate to PM if unresolved within 24 hours
- **Quality Standards**: All deliverables must pass QA validation before release

### 5.3 Recognition and Rewards

- **Spot Awards**: Monetary bonuses for exceptional contributions
- **Public Recognition**: Shout-outs in team meetings
- **Career Development**: Opportunities for certification and training
