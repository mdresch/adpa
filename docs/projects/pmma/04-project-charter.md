## 2. Purpose and Business Justification

### 2.1 Problem/Opportunity

Traditional project management training and documentation systems rely on static, siloed knowledge repositories that fail to adapt to real-world complexities. This results in:

- Prolonged time-to-mastery for new team members (average 6-12 months)
- Inconsistent documentation quality, leading to compliance risks and operational inefficiencies
- Limited real-time decision support, increasing project failure rates by 20-30% (PMI Pulse of the Profession, 2023)

PMMA addresses these gaps by transforming project management into a dynamic, data-driven "flight simulator" that compresses time-to-mastery, elevates documentation maturity, and establishes a self-reinforcing learning ecosystem.

### 2.2 Strategic Alignment

PMMA aligns with the following organizational OKRs:

1. Reduce onboarding time for project managers by 40% (from 6-12 months to 3-6 months)
2. Improve documentation maturity scores from "Basic" to "Advanced" (per PMBOK 7 standards)
3. Increase governance quality by 30% through real-time evidence-based decision support
4. Enhance operational efficiency by automating 50% of manual documentation reviews

### 2.3 Expected Benefits

| Benefit | Baseline | Target | Measurement Method |
|---------|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Time-to-Mastery | 6-12 months | 3-6 months | Onboarding completion time |
| Documentation Maturity | Basic (Level 1) | Advanced (Level 3) | PMBOK 7 Maturity Model |
| Governance Quality | 70% compliance | 95% compliance | Audit pass rate |
| Operational Efficiency | 20% manual reviews | 50% automation | Process automation rate |

### 2.4 High-Level Strategic Objectives

1. Transform project management into a dynamic learning ecosystem by converting real project documents into atomic semantic units
2. Compress time-to-mastery through adaptive learning algorithms and scenario-based training
3. Elevate documentation maturity via automated normalization and conflict resolution
4. Enhance governance quality with real-time evidence-based decision support
5. Establish a self-reinforcing learning engine where learners and the system co-evolve

## 5. Scope Overview & Boundaries

### 5.1 In-Scope

1. **Ingestion & Normalization Layer (ADPA)**: Automated extraction and normalization of project documents into atomic semantic units
2. **ECS Reasoning Core**: Authority, evidence, and temporal modeling for conflict resolution and decision support
3. **Documentation Mastery Engine (DME)**: Automated documentation quality assessment and improvement
4. **Scenario Generator & Decision Simulator**: Interactive training and real-time decision support
5. **Governance Knowledge Graph (GKG)**: Centralized, dynamic knowledge repository
6. **Pattern Library & Risk Signature Index**: Proactive risk identification and mitigation
7. **Mastery Scoring & Maturity Analytics**: Adaptive learning and performance tracking
8. **Feedback, Coaching & Explainability**: Personalized guidance and system transparency
9. **Ops & Compliance**: Auditability, data retention, and PII controls

### 5.2 Out-of-Scope

1. Custom development of third-party tools (e.g., Jira, MS Project)
2. End-user hardware procurement (e.g., laptops, servers)
3. Non-project management training (e.g., leadership development)
4. Legacy system migration (unless explicitly required for integration)

### 5.3 Major Deliverables
