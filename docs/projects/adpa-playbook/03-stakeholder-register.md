## 1. Executive Summary

The ADPA Playbook project aims to develop two comprehensive guidebooks—the ADPA Program Playbook and the Advanced Document Processing & Analytics Framework (ADPAF) Playbook—to standardize, govern, and scale the ADPA initiative.

This Stakeholder Register identifies, categorizes, and analyzes all key stakeholders, assessing their power, interest, and engagement strategies in alignment with PMBOK® Guide – Seventh Edition.

## 5. Stakeholder Management Plan

### 5.1 Engagement Process

1. **Identify Stakeholders:** Continuously identify and update stakeholders based on project evolution
2. **Analyze Stakeholders:** Assess power, interest, and influence using the Stakeholder Analysis Matrix
3. **Develop Engagement Strategies:** Tailor strategies based on stakeholder categorization
4. **Execute Engagement Plan:** Implement strategies through communication and collaboration
5. **Monitor and Adjust:** Regularly review effectiveness and adjust strategies

### 5.2 Tools and Techniques

| Tool/Technique | Purpose | Frequency |
|----------------|---------|-----------|
| Stakeholder Register | Document and track stakeholder information | Updated Monthly |
| Stakeholder Analysis Matrix | Visualize power/influence and interest | Reviewed Bi-weekly |
| Communication Plan | Define methods, frequency, and key messages | Updated Monthly |
| Governance Reviews | Formal updates to executive stakeholders | Monthly |
| CCB Meetings | Review and approve change requests | Bi-weekly |
| UAT and Pilot Feedback | Gather feedback from end-users | Monthly |
