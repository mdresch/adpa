## 1. Executive Summary

The ADPA Digital Twins iTwin IoT and Sensor Register project is a strategic initiative under ADPA's Digital Transformation Roadmap (2025–2030) to integrate generic Digital Twin Assets with iTwin Model Assets, enabling full 3D visualization of industrial environments. This project addresses critical gaps in ADPA's asset management ecosystem, including fragmented data sources, reactive maintenance strategies, and limited real-time situational awareness.

### Key Objectives

1. **Establish a Single Source of Truth (SSOT):** Consolidate IoT sensor data and 3D models into a unified platform for real-time monitoring and decision-making
2. **Enhance Operational Efficiency:** Reduce downtime by 30% through predictive maintenance and automated alerts
3. **Improve Decision-Making:** Provide teams with accurate, up-to-date 3D representations of physical assets
4. **Drive Innovation:** Enable scenario planning and simulation for asset lifecycle optimization

### Expected Benefits

- **Financial:** Cost savings from reduced downtime (target: €2.5M annually) and optimized maintenance spend (target: 15% reduction)
- **Non-Financial:** Improved collaboration across teams, enhanced regulatory compliance, and increased data accuracy
- **Strategic:** Position ADPA as a leader in Industry 4.0 adoption, supporting long-term digital transformation goals

### Realization Timeline

- **Immediate (Post-Go-Live):** System integration, user training, and baseline KPI establishment
- **Mid-Term (6–12 Months):** Predictive maintenance rollout, 3D visualization adoption, and process optimization
- **Long-Term (12+ Months):** Full asset lifecycle management, scenario planning, and continuous improvement

## 3. Benefit Profiles (Deep Dive)

### 3.1 Reduced Downtime (B1)

- **Description:** Minimize unplanned downtime through predictive maintenance and automated alerts
- **Metric/KPI:**
  - **Definition:** Percentage reduction in unplanned downtime
  - **Calculation Method:** (Baseline Downtime - Actual Downtime) / Baseline Downtime * 100
  - **Target Value:** 30% reduction by Q4 2026
  - **Current Baseline:** 120 hours/year (historical average)
  - **Measurement Source:** IoT sensor logs, maintenance records, and system alerts
  - **Frequency:** Quarterly
  - **Owner:** Operations Team

### 3.2 Optimized Maintenance Spend (B2)

- **Description:** Reduce maintenance costs through data-driven scheduling
- **Metric/KPI:**
  - **Definition:** Percentage reduction in maintenance expenditure
  - **Calculation Method:** (Baseline Maintenance Cost - Actual Cost) / Baseline Maintenance Cost * 100
  - **Target Value:** 15% reduction by Q4 2026
  - **Current Baseline:** €5M/year (historical average)
  - **Measurement Source:** Financial reports, work orders, and procurement data
  - **Frequency:** Quarterly
  - **Owner:** Maintenance Team

### 3.3 Improved Data Accuracy (B3)

- **Description:** Ensure high accuracy in asset tracking and reporting
- **Metric/KPI:**
  - **Definition:** Percentage of data records with ≥95% accuracy
  - **Calculation Method:** (Number of Accurate Records / Total Records) * 100
  - **Target Value:** 95% accuracy by Q3 2026
  - **Current Baseline:** 85% (current state)
  - **Measurement Source:** Data validation audits, system logs
  - **Frequency:** Monthly
  - **Owner:** Data Engineer

### 3.4 Enhanced Collaboration (B4)

- **Description:** Improve cross-functional collaboration between teams
- **Metric/KPI:**
  - **Definition:** Number of joint workshops and shared dashboards used
  - **Calculation Method:** Track adoption of collaboration tools
  - **Target Value:** 10 workshops/quarter and 80% dashboard adoption by Q4 2026
  - **Current Baseline:** 2 workshops/quarter, 30% adoption
  - **Measurement Source:** User activity logs, survey feedback
  - **Frequency:** Quarterly
  - **Owner:** Change Manager

### 3.5 Regulatory Compliance (B5)

- **Description:** Ensure compliance with industry standards
- **Metric/KPI:**
  - **Definition:** Percentage of compliant assets and audit pass rate
  - **Calculation Method:** (Number of Compliant Assets / Total Assets) * 100
  - **Target Value:** 100% compliance by Q2 2026
  - **Current Baseline:** 90% (current state)
  - **Measurement Source:** Compliance audits, regulatory reports
  - **Frequency:** Bi-annually
  - **Owner:** Regulatory Bodies

## 5. Dependencies and Risks

### 5.1 Dependencies

| Dependency | Impact on Benefits |
|------------|-------------------|
| Vendor (Bentley Systems) Support | Delays in iTwin platform integration may impact B1 (Reduced Downtime) and B7 (Situational Awareness) |
| IT Department Readiness | Insufficient infrastructure may delay B3 (Data Accuracy) and B5 (Compliance) |
| User Adoption | Low engagement may reduce B4 (Collaboration) and B6 (Scenario Planning) |

### 5.2 Risks to Realization

| Risk Description | Mitigation Strategy |
|------------------|---------------------|
| Data Integration Failures | Conduct pilot testing and vendor SLAs to ensure system reliability |
| Resistance to Change | Implement change management workshops and user feedback loops |
| Budget Overruns | Establish contingency funds and regular financial reviews |
| Regulatory Changes | Monitor industry updates and engage legal advisors for compliance |

## 6. Dis-Benefits (Negative Impacts)

| Impact | Affected Stakeholder | Mitigation Strategy |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------|

| System Downtime During Migration | Operations Team | Schedule off-peak migration and backup systems |
| Data Privacy Concerns | Regulatory Bodies | Implement GDPR-compliant data handling and user access controls |
