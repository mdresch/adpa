## 1. Executive Summary

The ADPA Digital Twins iTwin IoT and Sensor Register project is a strategic initiative to integrate generic Digital Twin Assets with iTwin Model Assets, enabling full 3D visualization of industrial environments. This project aligns with ADPA's Digital Transformation Roadmap (2025–2030) and leverages PMBOK 7 principles to ensure robust stakeholder engagement, governance, and value delivery.

### 1.1 Project Overview

- **Objective:** Establish a single source of truth for digital twin assets, enabling real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, and operational optimization
- **Key Benefits:**
  - Enhanced operational efficiency through real-time IoT sensor data integration
  - Improved decision-making with accurate 3D asset visualization
  - Reduced downtime via predictive maintenance capabilities
  - Increased cross-functional collaboration among engineering, operations, and maintenance teams
- **Strategic Alignment:** Supports ADPA's 2025–2030 Digital Transformation Roadmap by modernizing asset management and data-driven decision-making

### 1.2 Stakeholder Management Approach

This Stakeholder Register identifies, analyzes, and categorizes all project stakeholders using PMBOK 7's Stakeholder Performance Domain and BABOK v3's Stakeholder Analysis Techniques. Engagement strategies are tailored to Power/Interest quadrants, ensuring proactive communication, risk mitigation, and alignment with project objectives.

**Key Stakeholder Groups:**
- **Executive Leadership** (High Power, High Interest) – Strategic oversight and funding approval
- **Technical Teams** (Medium Power, High Interest) – Implementation and data integration
- **End Users** (Low Power, High Interest) – Operational adoption and feedback
- **Vendors & Regulatory Bodies** (High Power, Medium Interest) – Compliance and platform support

## 4. Engagement Strategy (PMBOK 7 & DMBOK2)

This section defines tailored engagement strategies for each stakeholder group, ensuring alignment with PMBOK 7's Stakeholder Engagement Principles and DMBOK2's Data Governance Frameworks.

### 4.1 Executive Stakeholders (High Power, High Interest)

**Stakeholders:** Project Sponsor, ADPA Executive Team, Project Steering Committee

**Engagement Strategy (PMBOK 7, Section 2.3.1):**

- **Strategic Alignment:** The Project Sponsor and ADPA Executive Team approve all major scope changes, budget adjustments, and strategic decisions
- **Communication Plan:**
  - Bi-weekly executive reviews (30-minute sessions)
  - Real-time escalation paths for critical issues
  - Personalized dashboards (Power BI) for real-time KPI tracking
- **Key Actions:**
  - Monthly steering committee meetings
  - Quarterly strategic alignment workshops

### 4.2 Technical & Data Stakeholders (Medium Power, High Interest)

**Stakeholders:** IT Department, Data Engineer, Software Developers, Bentley Systems (Vendor)

**Engagement Strategy (DMBOK2, Section 3.2):**

- **Data Governance & Integration:** Ensure seamless IoT sensor data integration with the iTwin platform
- **Communication Plan:**
  - Weekly technical syncs (60-minute sessions)
  - Vendor collaboration sessions with Bentley Systems
  - Dedicated Slack channel for real-time troubleshooting
- **Key Actions:**
  - Data validation workshops
  - Security compliance reviews (quarterly)
  - Performance benchmarking

### 4.3 End Users & Operational Teams (Low Power, High Interest)

**Stakeholders:** Engineers and Operators, Maintenance Team, Operations Team

**Engagement Strategy (PMBOK 7, Section 2.3.3):**

- **User Adoption & Training:** Ensure smooth adoption through structured training programs
- **Communication Plan:**
  - Monthly user feedback sessions
  - Quarterly training workshops
  - Dedicated support portal for troubleshooting
- **Key Actions:**
  - Pilot testing phase with 10% of end users
  - User satisfaction surveys (post-training)
  - Gamification incentives for active participation

### 4.4 Regulatory & Compliance Stakeholders (High Power, Medium Interest)

**Stakeholders:** Regulatory Bodies, Finance Department

**Engagement Strategy (DMBOK2, Section 3.2.2):**

- **Compliance & Financial Oversight:** Ensure adherence to budgetary constraints and industry standards
- **Communication Plan:**
  - Quarterly compliance audits
  - Monthly financial reviews
  - Dedicated compliance officer
- **Key Actions:**
  - GDPR compliance workshops
  - Cost-benefit analysis reports
  - Regulatory impact assessments
