## 2. Purpose and Business Justification

### 2.1 Problem/Opportunity

ADPA currently lacks a unified platform for integrating generic Digital Twin Assets with iTwin Model Assets, resulting in fragmented data visualization and limited operational insights. This gap hinders real-time decision-making, predictive maintenance, and asset lifecycle management. The ADPA Digital Twins iTwin IoT and Sensor Register project addresses this by creating a single source of truth for digital twin assets, enabling full 3D visualization of industrial environments.

### 2.2 Strategic Alignment

This project aligns with ADPA's Digital Transformation Roadmap (2025–2030) and supports the following strategic objectives:

- **Operational Excellence:** Enhance efficiency through real-time data integration
- **Innovation:** Leverage advanced digital technologies for predictive maintenance and scenario planning
- **Collaboration:** Improve cross-functional teamwork between engineering, operations, and maintenance teams

### 2.3 Expected Benefits

- **Operational Efficiency:** Reduce downtime by 15% through predictive maintenance
- **Decision-Making:** Improve accuracy of operational decisions with real-time 3D visualization
- **Cost Savings:** Achieve $2M in annual savings through optimized asset management
- **Compliance:** Ensure adherence to regulatory standards for data privacy and industrial safety

### 2.4 High-Level Strategic Objectives

1. Establish a unified platform for integrating IoT sensor data with 3D modeling
2. Enable real-time monitoring and simulation of physical infrastructure
3. Support predictive maintenance and operational optimization
4. Enhance collaboration across engineering, operations, and maintenance teams
5. Achieve 95% accuracy in digital twin asset representation

### 2.5 Value Realization

Benefits will be tracked post-deployment using the following metrics:

- **Operational Efficiency:** Reduction in downtime and maintenance costs
- **Decision-Making:** Improvement in response time to operational issues
- **Cost Savings:** Annual savings from optimized asset management
- **User Adoption:** Percentage of end-users actively utilizing the platform

## 5. Scope Overview & Boundaries

### 5.1 In-Scope

- Integration of generic Digital Twin Assets with iTwin Model Assets
- Real-time monitoring and visualization of IoT sensor data
- Predictive maintenance capabilities using IoT data
- User training and change management for end-users
- Compliance with regulatory standards for data privacy and industrial safety

### 5.2 Out-of-Scope

- Development of new IoT sensors or hardware
- Integration with non-ADPA systems or third-party platforms not approved by the Steering Committee
- Full-scale deployment beyond the pilot phase without Steering Committee approval

### 5.3 Major Deliverables

- **Digital Twin Integration Platform:** Unified platform for 3D visualization and real-time monitoring
- **Predictive Maintenance Module:** Capabilities for forecasting maintenance needs using IoT data
- **User Training Materials:** Documentation and training sessions for end-users
- **Compliance Documentation:** Reports and audits for regulatory compliance

### 5.4 External Interfaces

- **Bentley iTwin Platform:** Integration with Bentley Systems for 3D modeling
- **ADPA IT Systems:** Integration with existing ADPA infrastructure for data management
- **IoT Sensors:** Real-time data feed from IoT sensors deployed across ADPA facilities
