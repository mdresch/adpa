# AI Driven Risk Management Resolutions 2026

## Project Overview

The **ADPA AI Driven Risk Management Resolutions 2026** initiative represents a transformative leap for Advanced Digital Processing Analytics (ADPA) in leveraging artificial intelligence to enhance project risk management, issue escalation, and resolution processes.

This project aligns with PMBOK 8 principles and integrates cutting-edge AI technologies to predict, flag, and resolve risks and issues with unprecedented efficiency and accuracy.

## Core Pillars

1. **AI-Driven Risk Management** - Predictive analytics, automated risk flagging, scenario simulation
2. **Automated Issue Escalation** - AI-powered playbooks, dynamic escalation workflows, real-time alerts
3. **AI-Powered Issue Management** - NLP for issue triage, RCA automation, knowledge base integration
4. **Guidance Toward Resolutions** - AI-generated resolution paths, decision support dashboards, proactive impact mitigation

## Key Objectives

- Reduce project disruptions by 40% through predictive risk analytics
- Improve issue resolution time by 30% via automated triage and escalation
- Enhance compliance with ISO/IEC 23894, NIST AI RMF, and EU AI Act
- Increase stakeholder satisfaction with real-time visibility
- Drive cost savings of 15-20% through early intervention
