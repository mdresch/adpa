# Business Case: ADPA AI Driven Risk Management Resolutions 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Project Overview

- **Project Name**: ADPA AI Driven Risk Management Resolutions 2026
- **Business Sponsor**: Menno Drescher (Project Sponsor)
- **Prepared By**: Senior Strategic Business Architect
- **Date**: December 22, 2025

The ADPA AI Driven Risk Management Resolutions 2026 initiative is a strategic investment designed to revolutionize Advanced Digital Processing Analytics' (ADPA) approach to project risk management, issue escalation, and resolution. This project aligns with PMBOK® Guide (7th Edition) principles and leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance predictive capabilities, automate workflows, and minimize project disruptions.

The initiative focuses on four core pillars:
1. **AI-Driven Risk Management**
2. **Automated Issue Escalation**
3. **AI-Powered Issue Management**
4. **Guidance Toward Resolutions and Impact Minimization**

### 1.2 Business Need and Value Proposition

The core business problem addressed by this initiative is the inefficiency and ineffectiveness of ADPA's current risk and issue management processes. Currently, ADPA relies on manual processes and siloed systems, leading to:

- Increased project disruptions (30% of projects experiencing delays)
- Higher operational costs from manual processes
- Compliance risks from lack of transparency
- Lost revenue opportunities from delayed risk identification

**Cost of Inaction**: Estimated at **$2.1 million annually**, comprising:
- $1.2 million in lost productivity
- $500,000 in compliance fines and penalties
- $400,000 in lost revenue

**Projected Financial Impact**:
- 40% reduction in project disruptions → $840,000 annual savings
- 30% improvement in issue resolution time → $360,000 annual savings
- $200,000 annual revenue growth
- $150,000 annual cost avoidance

Over a 5-year horizon: **NPV of $3.2 million** and **ROI of 180%**

### 1.3 Recommendation

Based on the analysis, we recommend proceeding with **Option 3: Full AI-Driven Risk Management Suite**, which delivers the highest Net Value ($3.2 million over 5 years) and aligns most closely with ADPA's strategic objectives.

## 3. Solution Options (Strategy Analysis)

### 3.1 Option 1: Status Quo (Do Nothing)

Continue with current manual processes.

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| No upfront investment | High ongoing operational costs |
| Minimal disruption to workflows | Increased project disruptions |
| No training required | Compliance risks |

**5-Year Total Cost**: $10,500,000

### 3.2 Option 2: Hybrid AI Solution (Partial Automation)

Implement COTS AI tools with limited customization.

**5-Year Total Cost**: $4,300,000  
**5-Year Benefits**: $6,300,000  
**Net Value**: $2,000,000

### 3.3 Option 3: Full AI-Driven Risk Management Suite (Recommended)

Fully integrated, custom AI-driven solution.

**Features**:
1. Predictive Risk Analytics with scenario simulation
2. Automated Issue Escalation with dynamic workflows
3. AI-Powered Issue Management with NLP and RCA
4. Resolution Guidance with decision support dashboards

**5-Year Total Cost**: $3,100,000  
**5-Year Benefits**: $8,400,000  
**Net Value**: $5,300,000  
**ROI**: 342%  
**NPV**: $3,200,000  
**Payback Period**: 2.1 years
