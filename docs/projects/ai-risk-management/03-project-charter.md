## 2. Purpose and Business Justification

### 2.1 Problem/Opportunity

ADPA currently faces significant challenges in project risk management:
- **Delayed risk identification**: Manual processes result in late detection
- **Inefficient issue triage**: Manual categorization causes escalation delays
- **Reactive resolution strategies**: Lack of predictive analytics leads to reactive management
- **Compliance risks**: Non-compliance with global governance standards

### 2.2 Strategic Alignment

- Digital Transformation
- Operational Excellence
- Compliance and Governance
- Innovation

### 2.3 Expected Benefits

- **Financial**: Reduce project disruptions by 40%, ~$2M annual savings
- **Operational**: Improve issue resolution time by 30%
- **Customer**: Increase stakeholder satisfaction
- **Compliance**: Ensure adherence to global governance standards
