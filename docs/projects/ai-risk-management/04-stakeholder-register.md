## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Project Overview

The ADPA AI Driven Risk Management Resolutions 2026 initiative is a transformative project to revolutionize risk management through AI-driven automation. This Stakeholder Register identifies, analyzes, and strategizes engagement for 22 key stakeholders, ensuring alignment with PMBOK 7's Stakeholder Performance Domain.

### 1.2 Stakeholder Engagement Objectives

- Ensure 100% stakeholder awareness of project goals by February 15, 2026
- Achieve 90%+ engagement from high-power/high-interest stakeholders
- Mitigate resistance from operational teams via training workshops
- Align AI ethics governance with ISO/IEC 23894 and EU AI Act
