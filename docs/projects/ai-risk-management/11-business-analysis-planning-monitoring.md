## 1. Executive Summary

This plan establishes the framework for identifying, analyzing, and managing business requirements throughout the project lifecycle, aligned with PMBOK 7 principles and BABOK v3 standards.

**Key Objectives**:
- Reduce risk identification time by 60% (target: <5 days)
- Automate 70% of issue triage processes
- Improve resolution time by 30% (target: <48 hours for critical issues)
- Achieve 95% stakeholder satisfaction with AI transparency
