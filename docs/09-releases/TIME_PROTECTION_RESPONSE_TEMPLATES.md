# Time Protection Response Templates

```
For your request, I recommend:
1. 📚 Check our comprehensive documentation (240+ pages): [link to docs]
2. 📝 Submit a Change Request to our Product Owner for prioritization: [email]
3. 🕒 Join Friday office hours (3-5 PM) for technical Q&A: [Slack #adpa-office-hours]

If this is truly urgent and can't wait, please escalate through [Product Owner] or [Technical Lead] for priority assessment.

Here's a one-pager about what I'm working on: [link to one-pager]

Thanks for understanding!
[Your Name]
```

## Tone Guidelines

**Always**:
- ✅ Be polite and professional
- ✅ Acknowledge their need
- ✅ Provide alternatives
- ✅ Explain the business context
- ✅ Thank them for understanding

**Never**:
- ❌ Be dismissive or rude
- ❌ Say "I'm too busy" (say "fully allocated to P0 work")
- ❌ Apologize excessively (you're doing your job)
- ❌ Commit to "I'll try to find time" (false promise)
- ❌ Blame the requester

---

## Escalation Ladder

**Level 1**: Use templates above (handles 80% of cases)  
**Level 2**: CC Product Owner on response (repeat requesters)  
**Level 3**: Product Owner intervention (violations after CC)  
**Level 4**: Technical Lead + HR (continued boundary violations)  

---

**Remember**: Protecting your time isn't selfish - it's how you deliver the $460K-$1M value ADPA promises. Your focused work benefits the entire organization!
