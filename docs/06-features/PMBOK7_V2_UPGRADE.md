## 📊 **New Structure**

### **1. Project Context and Value Proposition**
**Focus**: WHY the project exists (PMBOK 7 Principle: VALUE)
- Business value, not just deliverables
- Strategic alignment
- Measurable outcomes

### **2. Development Approach and Tailoring** ⭐ **NEW SECTION!**
**Focus**: Justification for Predictive/Agile/Hybrid approach
- Why this approach for this project?
- Connection to Uncertainty, Planning, and Delivery domains
- Organizational standards adapted

### **3. Project Performance Domains Strategy** ⭐ **CORE CHANGE!**

**8 Domains** (replaces 10 Knowledge Areas):

#### **3.1 Stakeholders**
- Principle: ENGAGEMENT
- Proactive collaboration, not just control
- Stakeholder matrix with engagement strategies

#### **3.2 Team**
- Principles: TEAM, LEADERSHIP, STEWARDSHIP
- Team culture, empowerment, agreements
- Psychological safety and collaboration

#### **3.3 Planning**
- Principle: ADAPTABILITY & RESILIENCE
- Adaptive vs. predictive planning
- Baselines (Scope, Schedule, Cost)

#### **3.4 Project Work**
- Principle: SYSTEMS THINKING
- Resource and process management
- Integration across domains

#### **3.5 Delivery**
- Principles: VALUE & QUALITY
- Outcomes over outputs
- Incremental value delivery
- Built-in quality

#### **3.6 Measurement**
- Principle: DEMONSTRATE VALUE
- KPIs linked to objectives
- EVM for predictive, burn-down for agile
- Business value metrics (ROI, benefit realization)

#### **3.7 Uncertainty** ⭐ **EXPANDED!**
- Principle: UNCERTAINTY & COMPLEXITY
- Not just "risks" but:
  - Risk (threats/opportunities)
  - Ambiguity (unclear requirements)
  - Complexity (dependencies, unknowns)
  - Volatility (rapid changes)

#### **3.8 Tailoring Summary**
- Cross-cutting review
- All adaptations documented

### **4. Subsidiary Plans** (Condensed)
- Scope, Schedule, Cost, Quality, Resource, Communications, Procurement
- Now supporting sections, not the main focus

### **5. Baselines and PMB**
- Performance Measurement Baseline
- Change control

### **6. Change Management**
- CCB for predictive baselines
- Lightweight for agile backlogs

### **7. Closure and Stewardship**
- Principle: STEWARDSHIP
- Lessons learned, knowledge transfer
