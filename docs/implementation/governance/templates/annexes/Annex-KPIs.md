# Annex C – KPI Catalogue

> KPIs align to PMBOK Measurement domain and feed ADPA analytics (quality dashboards). 

## Project Health
- Schedule Variance (SV), Cost Variance (CV), Scope Stability Index, Test Pass Rate, Defect Density

## OCM & Adoption
- Training Completion %, Readiness Index, Adoption %, CSAT, Ticket Volume Trend (T+30/T+60)

## Business Value
- Inventory Turnover, Days to Close, OTIF, Margin %, DSO/Working Capital, NPS
