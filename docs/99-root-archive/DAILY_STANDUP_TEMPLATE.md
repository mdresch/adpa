## 📝 **How to Use This Template**

1. **Copy this file** each day with the new date
2. **Each agent fills in their section** by their local 10 AM or scheduled time
3. **Team Lead reviews** by 11 AM and addresses blockers
4. **Archive** completed standups in `standup-archive/` folder
5. **Update main tracker** (`AGENT_PROGRESS_TRACKER.md`) weekly

**File Naming:** `STANDUP_YYYY-MM-DD.md` (e.g., `STANDUP_2025-11-04.md`)
