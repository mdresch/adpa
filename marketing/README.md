## 💡 Key Success Factors

### 1. **Consistency > Volume**
- Better: 2 great posts/week for 12 weeks
- Worse: 7 mediocre posts/week for 2 weeks then ghost

### 2. **Engagement > Broadcasting**
- Spend 2x as much time engaging as posting
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours
- Start conversations, don't just promote

### 3. **Value > Selling**
- 80% education/insights, 20% promotion
- Show real results, not marketing claims
- Be transparent about challenges

### 4. **Patience > Rushing**
- Results take 60-90 days minimum
- Don't judge performance before Day 90
- Compound growth starts Month 3+

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

### Common Mistakes:

1. **Posting everywhere at once** → Focus 90% on LinkedIn
2. **Selling in every post** → Provide value first
3. **Ignoring comments** → Engagement = algorithm boost
4. **Inconsistent posting** → Consistency beats intensity
5. **No call-to-action** → Always guide next step
6. **Generic content** → Use your specific metrics (600x, etc.)
7. **Quitting too early** → Need 90 days minimum
