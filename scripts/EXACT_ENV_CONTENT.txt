=========================================

1. Go to: https://console.upstash.com
2. Sign in
3. Click on database: "natural-vulture-7034"
4. Look for "Connect" or "Details" section
5. Find "Redis Connect" URL
6. It shows: rediss://default:PASSWORD@natural-vulture-7034.upstash.io:6379
7. Copy the PASSWORD part (between "default:" and "@")
8. Paste it in server/.env line 11

Method 2: Railway Variables (If linked)
1. Go to: https://railway.app/dashboard
2. Select ADPA project
3. Click backend service
4. Variables tab
5. Click REDIS_URL to reveal
6. Copy password from the URL
